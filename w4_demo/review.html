Trivia

  • The total amount of cigarettes John Constantine smokes in total is 13.In several cultures, 13 can be a lucky or unlucky number. When Constantine smoked those 13 cigarettes, the message that the movie was giving that John Constantine is a double edged sword.
  • The actual pronunciation of Constantine’s name ends with TINE not TEEN contrary to popular belief.
  • In the begining of the movie, the spear of destiny is found wrapped around a nazi flag. The spear is a facsimile of the "Holy Lance" is said to be a historical artifact that pierced Jesus while on the cross.
  • Constantine slits his wrists to summon the Devil in order to have him stop Mammon. The devil grabs him with his left hand. Constantine also slits his wrists with the left side. It's a common theory that the left side is "evil" or "sinister".
